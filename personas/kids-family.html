The Parent Promise

We're parents too. Here's exactly what goes into (and stays out of) our bakes.

What Goes In ✓

  • Real butter from local dairies
  • Free-range eggs
  • Natural vanilla & cocoa
  • Organic honey where sweetened
  • Clean flour, no bleaching agents
  • Natural fruit extracts for flavour

What Stays Out ✗

  • Artificial colours & dyes
  • Preservatives of any kind
  • Trans fats & hydrogenated oils
  • High fructose corn syrup
  • Artificial flavouring agents
  • Chemical raising agents
